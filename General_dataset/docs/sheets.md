# ESG Sheets Documentation

This document describes the structure and layout of the ESG Dashboard sheets generated by `sheets_esg.py`.

## Sheet 1: Portfolio Overview

**Goal**: Provide a high-level summary of the portfolio's ESG performance.

### Visuals
1.  **Total Emissions (KPI)**: Displays the aggregate CO2 emissions.
2.  **Emissions by Sector (Bar Chart)**: Breakdown of emissions across different sectors.
3.  **Sector Composition (Pie Chart)**: Shows the weight or emission share of each sector.
4.  **Emissions Over Time (Line Chart)**: Trend analysis of emissions over available years.
5.  **Holdings Table**: Detailed list of assets with their specific emission data.

### Layout
The sheet uses a grid layout (30 columns wide).

- **Header**: Title spanning the top.
- **Top Row**:
    - Left: KPI (Total Emissions)
    - Center: Bar Chart (Sector Breakdown)
    - Right: Pie Chart (Composition)
- **Bottom Row**:
    - Left: Line Chart (Trends)
    - Right: Table (Details)

## Sheet 2: Risk & Exposure

**Goal**: Analyze specific ESG risks and geographical/sectoral exposure.

### Visuals
1.  **Heatmap**: Cross-analysis of Sector vs Year (or other dimensions) to spot hotspots.
2.  **Treemap**: Hierarchical view of the portfolio, sized by exposure/emissions and colored by intensity/score.
3.  **Gauge**: Comparison of current Carbon Intensity against a target (e.g., Paris Agreement alignment).
4.  **Geographical Map**: Filled map showing exposure by Country.

### Layout
- **Header**: Title spanning the top.
- **Top Row**:
    - Left: Heatmap
    - Right: Treemap
- **Bottom Row**:
    - Left: Gauge Chart
    - Right: Geographical Map
